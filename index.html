We're recruiting!


The wait is finally over. VIThink an AI student research group is now open for inquisitive minds and people who can work with us on industry level problems which we get from our partons.


What do we expect from applicants?

We expect applications to have an inquisitive mind and the willingness to learn along with basic programming knowledge and mathematics. Having Knowledge on the basics of machine learning and related topics is a plus.

What you'll be doing?

You'll be working on a realtime industry level problem statement which is particularly assigned to us by our patrons. Apart from projects we have several self learning groups etc. in VIThink which you can join and boost your skillset with our in-house experts.
