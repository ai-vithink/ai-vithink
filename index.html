Hola!

We are a student run research and development group, with the stated aim of promoting interdisciplinary AI research among undergrads. We are tinkering with all things AI -- from vanilla Machine Learning to Generative Adversarial Networks, and everything in between. VIThink was founded in the summer of 2018 by the visionary Dr. Susan Elias with a team of 15 passionate undergrads at the Vellore Institute of Technology, Chennai Campus.

United by the dream of intelligent machines, and armed with a zealous thirst for knowledge, we are set out to create, to apply, to contribute and to think.
