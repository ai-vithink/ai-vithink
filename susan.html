“Research is the creation of knowledge” - said  Neil Amstrong who took one small step on the moon one day to signify a giant  leap for mankind. “Imagination is more important than knowledge” said Albert Einstein the creator of  the world famous equation E = MC2.  While we contemplate over  ‘knowledge through research’ and ‘power of imagination’ we also become aware that passion and perseverance  are in addition the driving forces behind any accomplishment especially in research and innovation -Susan Elias
